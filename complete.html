What comes next

This is the operating rhythm

Completing the program means ESC is ready to support committees with a clear, consistent model: consult, tier risk, route expertise, close the loop, and standardize what works.

ESC stays available

Standing check-ins, facilitation support, action logs, and playbooks help committees maintain momentum without creating extra burden.

Committees keep ownership

Each committee keeps the decision, the credit, and the UBC report-back. ESC supports the process so the work is easier to sustain.

Bottom line: ESC is the support structure that helps VNC ICU UBC committees move from idea → risk-aware plan → action → visible follow-up, without taking the work away from the committee that owns it.
