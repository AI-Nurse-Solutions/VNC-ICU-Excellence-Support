Step 1

Finding Our Footing Together

Everything good starts with a shared sense of why. In this first step, we'll make your purpose feel real and doable, picture together what success can look like, and settle into a few gentle guardrails — so every team feels both safe and free to lead.

Part 1 · Your Purpose, Made Real

Turning "why" into something you can hold

Your committee exists to lift others up — and that purpose comes alive the moment it turns into something you can actually do. Together, we'll shape it into small, clear commitments your team feels proud to own.

  • Say your purpose in plain words everyone can feel.
  • Name the real ways your team supports and lifts up others.
  • Turn each one into a gentle commitment someone's glad to carry.

Part 2 · Picturing Success Together

Imagining the good that's possible

It's easier to head somewhere when you can picture it. So we paint that picture together — a unit where teams support each other freely — and ground it in a real moment, so you'll recognize success when you feel it.

What this can look like: A unit-practice committee runs into a staffing-ratio snag. Instead of waiting and worrying, they gather for a 30-minute chat, weigh the risk together, and have a safe plan in place that same week — proud of what they built, with us cheering alongside.

Part 3 · Gentle Guardrails

The safety net that lets you leap

Guardrails aren't there to box you in — they're there so you can move boldly without fear. We share four simple, caring principles any team can lean on from day one.
