Step 2

Growing Stronger, Together

None of us is as strong as all of us. This step is about leaning on each other's gifts, solving problems shoulder to shoulder with other teams, and building kind, steady rhythms of feedback — so every committee keeps growing in its own way.

Part 1 · Leaning on Each Other's Strengths

The right person, gently brought in

Chances are, the wisdom you need is already in the room. We help notice the strengths your team already holds and connect each question to the right person — always staying a step behind the team that owns the decision.

  • Notice and honor the strengths already in your committee.
  • Bring in the right person for the moment, then step back.
  • We're a helping hand, never a stand-in — the call stays yours.

Part 2 · Solving Problems Side by Side

Better together, with everyone's voice

Some challenges are easier to carry when more hands are on them. We offer a simple, friendly way to bring teams together — so everyone contributes, everyone's heard, and no one loses their voice in the room.

A working-together session, in four easy beats: name the shared challenge, share what each team is up against, dream up options together, and leave with next steps everyone feels good about.

Part 3 · Kind, Steady Feedback

Growing a little, together, all the time

Growth feels gentler when it's regular and kind. We help set up easy rhythms — short check-ins and just a few meaningful signs of progress — so your team keeps getting better, with us close by and rooting for you.

  • Pick a few signs of progress that truly matter to you.
  • Choose short, warm check-ins over rare, heavy reviews.
  • Carry every lesson — and every win — into the next round.
