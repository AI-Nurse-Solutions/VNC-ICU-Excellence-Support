Module 3

Facilitative Guidance & Collaborative Planning

The final step turns support into a habit. This module hands committees the templates, the session structure, and the ongoing rhythm they need to plan and execute — while keeping full agency throughout.

Section 1 · Guidance Templates

Templates that prompt teams to lead

We introduce adaptable templates that prompt committees to formulate their own plans. The template asks the questions; the committee writes the answers and steers the process.

  • Start from a structured prompt, not a blank page.
  • Adapt each template to the committee's real context.
  • The team fills it in — the plan is theirs from the first line.

Section 2 · Collaborative Planning Sessions

Facilitate; don't dictate

We structure sessions where our committee facilitates and supports other teams as they develop their own operational plans. We hold the process; they hold the decisions.

Our role in the room: set the agenda, keep time, ask the sharpening questions, and capture decisions — so the committee can focus entirely on the plan they own.

Section 3 · Continuous Empowerment

Support that stays, agency that grows

We establish mechanisms for ongoing facilitative support, so committees retain full agency as they execute. The goal is independence that strengthens over time — not dependence on us.

Standing check-ins Shared playbooks On-call facilitation Growing autonomy

Program complete. A committee that has worked through all three modules shares a clear purpose, collaborates with agency intact, and can plan and execute with support on call. That's shared governance, amplified.
