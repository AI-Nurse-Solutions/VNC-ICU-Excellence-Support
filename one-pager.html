Mission: make VNC ICU committee work clearer, safer, and easier to sustain.

The Excellence Support Committee is the facilitation and infrastructure partner for VNC ICU UBC committees. We equip, guide, and elevate committees with practical tools, risk guardrails, expertise routing, and follow-through — while each committee keeps ownership of its decisions and work.

Onboarding — three practical steps
Step 1

Define the ESC Role

Clarify mission, boundaries, and what success looks like inside the VNC ICU UBC structure.

Step 2

Practice the Support Method

Route expertise, facilitate cross-committee work, and close the loop through simple feedback cycles.

Step 3

Build the Toolkit

Use templates, planning sessions, action logs, and check-ins to make progress visible and repeatable.

Methodology
  • Consult first: understand the committee need and desired outcome.
  • Tier the risk: Green, Yellow, or Red for action, UBC discussion, or escalation.
  • Route expertise: bring in the right clinical, operational, educator, quality, or leadership voice.
  • Close the loop: track action, outcomes, barriers, and UBC follow-up.
  • Standardize what works: turn repeatable practice into templates and playbooks.
